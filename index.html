Born from the Tundra

Frost Front Dayz was founded by a group of DayZ veterans tired of unreliable hosting, bloated packages, and zero support. We knew there was a better way.

We started by running our own community server in 2019 and grew into one of the most trusted DayZ hosting and build services in the community. Every package and build is tested by actual players.

We don't just sell server space. We craft experiences. Whether you want brutal survival or a thriving community hub, Frost Front Dayz delivers.
